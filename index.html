About Me

My name is Adrian Bece. I am a Frontend Developer specializing in eCommerce. I am currently working at Inchoo - a full service eCommerce agency. I live in Osijek, Croatia. I graduated at Faculty of Electrical Engineering in Osijek in 2015 where I've earned the Master's degree in computer science. In 2016 I have became a Certified Magento Frontend Developer.

I have worked at several Magento and Wordpress projects while working at Inchoo. Previously, I have worked on several notable personal projects ranging from website specializing of ordering customized pizza to various simple games and image-processing software.
