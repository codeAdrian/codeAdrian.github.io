Jamie Mallender



Since 2009. I've been working with a British musician Jamie Mallender who previously worked with members of Black Sabbath, Tony Martin, Ravenlord, T-Rex, etc. I've created cd covers and promo materials for his 2 albums: "Return to bass" and "Tunes from the mothership" released in 2009. and 2011., respectively.

Jamie has been a great friend of mine and fellow music-buff ever since we started working together. He gave me the job that kickstarted my interest in graphic design and I've kept honing my skills ever since. You can check out his albums and music by clicking on the buttons below.

Misc.

RavenLord - "Black Friar" lyrics video


In 2013. I've made a lyrics video for an international heavy metal band "Raven Lord". I've used Camtasia to animate the images that I've made in Photoshop and make do the transition between them using Camtasia's special effects. Also I've used official images and cover art from band's official artist and designer to create this video.

The band and their management liked the video and they've uploaded it to their official youtube and posted it on their official social media channels.

IEEE - Student Branch Osijek



During 2012. and 2013. I've been a member of IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) and an Osijek branch treasurer for IEEE. During that time I've been tasked to create promo posters for various educational events and t-shirt designs. Educational events were held by experienced employees from various software and web development companies from Osijek like Inchoo, Betaware and Mono.

I've used photoshop to make a general template and I've change it halfway through the year to something more simple and modern.
